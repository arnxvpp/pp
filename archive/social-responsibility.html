Social Responsibility

We believe in the immense power of storytellers and thought leaders to have positive and lasting social impact. We see it as a responsibility to use our unique position, through which so much of popular culture flows, to ignite and champion efforts to improve the world around us.

From helping to ensure that all voices are heard and amplified, including those that have historically been marginalized, to improving economic and social conditions in our local communities and worldwide, has for more than a years contributed resources, expertise, time and funds across a range of critical issues.

Foundation
Created in 2024, ’s philanthropic arm has become an integral part of its culture. Harnessing the power and reach of the entertainment and sports industries to create positive social change, the Foundation assists clients in building public awareness campaigns and forging strategic partnerships; encourages volunteerism among employees and throughout our communities; and provides financial contributions and in-kind donations to important causes. The Foundation focuses its efforts on education, the environment, human rights, social justice, and relief after disaster. 

Diversity & Inclusion
More diverse voices, from diverse backgrounds, provide a richer and better experience for audiences worldwide. has long focused on ensuring that historically underrepresented groups within the talent community are assured equal opportunity for success.  To this end, we have created events, workshops and conferences to help generate opportunities – and address the challenges – for emerging and established talent; conducted research and reported findings on the correlation between diversity and business success; focused on increasing the diversity of our own client base; and advocated directly with marketplace buyers on the value of diverse voices.

Corporate Social Responsibility Consulting
Recognizing the tremendous impact companies – with their brand voice and business commitments – can have on global communities; and the growing importance of a company’s social purpose to their consumers, employees and stakeholders, ’s Social Impact business provides strategic consulting services to public and private sector companies. We help them develop, implement, and market their philanthropic, social responsibility, and purpose-driven initiatives to achieve maximum social impact and business returns.
